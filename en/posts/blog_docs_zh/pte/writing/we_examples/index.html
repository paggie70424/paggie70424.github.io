法律改变人的行为 #101003 极高频 极限预测

Some people think human behaviors can be changed by laws, while others think laws have little effect. What is your opinion. 一些人认为法律可以限制人的行为，另一些人认为法律没有什么作用。你的意见是什么?

观点1：可以限制

Personally speaking, human behaviors can be changed by laws.

  • 论点1：防止犯罪
    • Laws can prevent people from taking part in crime.
    • 参考论证1：法律 – 惩罚 – 避免犯罪
      • [legislations] – [punishments and penalties] – [reduced criminal behavior]
    • 参考论证2：法律 – 定义犯罪行为 – 避免犯法
      • [rules and regulations] – [definition of criminal behaviors] – [the avoidance of crime]
  • 论点2：正确价值观
    • Laws can help shape citizens’ sense of morality and influence their behavior.
    • 参考论证1：法律 – 定义道德标准 – 道德行为
      • [laws and legislations] – [moral standards] – [ethical conduct]
    • 参考论证2：法律 – 清晰的行为准则 – 遵守法律的行为
      • [rules and regulations] – [clear behavioral boundaries] – [lawful compliance]
  • 论点3：承担社会责任
    • Laws can guide people to assume their social responsibilities.
    • 参考论证1：法律 – 建立社会责任感 – 对社会负责的行为
      • [laws and legislations] – [a sense of public duty] – [responsible conduct]
    • 参考论证2：法律 – 法律义务 – 实现法律责任
      • [rules and regulations] – [legal obligations] – [the fulfilment of civic duties]
观点2：不能限制

Personally speaking, laws have little effect on human behaviors.

  • 论点1：法律约束无法限制内在动机
    • Legal control have limited capacity to regulate people’s internal motivations and behaviors.
    • 参考论证1：法律 – 建立社会责任感 – 对社会负责的行为
      • [laws and legislation] – [external constraints] – [limited influence on personal choices]
    • 参考论证2：法律 – 法律义务 – 实现法律责任
      • [rules and regulation] – [limited fear of punishiment] – [temporary compliance]
  • 论点2：法律缺乏执行力
    • Law’s limited enforcement reduces the ability to regulate behavior.
    • 参考论证1：法律 – 执行力有限 – 震慑作用低
      • [laws and legislation] – [enforcement gaps] – [low deterrent effect]
    • 参考论证2：法律 – 监管能力不足 – 规避合法行为
      • [rules and regulation] – [weak supervision] – [rule avoidance]
  • 论点3：法律不能预防犯罪行为
    • Laws are unable to prevent legal violation.
    • 参考论证1：法律 – 以惩罚为主的机制 – 预防作用有限
      • [laws and legislation] – [punishment-based measures] – [weak preventive effect]
    • 参考论证2：法律 – 事后型执行方式 – 行为预防能力不足
      • [rules and regulation] – [reactive enforcement] – [limited behavioral prevention]

  • Paragraph 1 — Introduction

    In today’s rapidly evolving world, many issues continue to attract public attention and debate. One such issue is whether human behaviors can be changed by laws, or if laws have little effect. While different people hold different opinions regarding this matter, my view is that human behaviors can indeed be changed by laws. This essay will discuss the key aspects of the topic and explain why this perspective is reasonable through logical arguments and relevant examples.

  • Paragraph 2 — First Main Argument

    To begin with, one major aspect of legal influence is its ability to prevent people from taking part in crime. This means that through legislations, punishments, and penalties, the legal system can lead to reduced criminal behavior. For instance, rules and regulations that provide a clear definition of criminal behaviors help citizens ensure the avoidance of crime, which clearly illustrates the importance of this factor. Therefore, this argument strongly supports the idea that laws serve as an effective deterrent.

  • Paragraph 3 — Second Main Argument

    Secondly, another significant consideration is that laws can help shape citizens’ sense of morality and influence their behavior. This is because laws and legislations define moral standards which lead to ethical conduct. For example, rules and regulations establishing clear behavioral boundaries result in lawful compliance demonstrates how this aspect affects the issue. Even though some may argue that legal controls have limited capacity to regulate internal motivations, the overall evidence suggests that laws guide people to assume their social responsibilities.

  • Paragraph 4 — Conclusion

    In conclusion, the impact of laws on human behavior is a complex issue with multiple perspectives. After examining the main arguments and supporting evidence, it is clear that laws are effective in preventing crime and shaping moral standards. Ultimately, adopting a thoughtful and balanced understanding of this topic will help individuals and society make better decisions in the future. +1

员工参与决策的利弊 #101004 极高频 极限预测

In some companies, some employers involve employees in the decision-making process of products and services. What are the advantages and disadvantages of such a policy? 有些雇主会让员工参与产品和服务的决策过程。这种政策的优点和缺点是什么?

观点：既有优点，也有缺点

Personally speaking, I firmly believe that there are both advantages and disadvantages of such a policy.

  • 论点1：增加员工忠诚度
    • Employees’ participation in the decision-making process of products and services can result in high employee loyalty.
    • 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 工作参与度 – 归属感
      • [employees making decisions] – [more engagement in work] – [a sense of belonging]
    • 参考论证2：员工参与决策 – 动力和热情 – 忠诚度
      • [involving employees in the decision-making process] – [their motivation and passion] – [higher employment loyalty]
  • 论点2：提高工作表现
    • Involving employees in the decision-making process of products and services will lead to better business performance.
    • 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 更好的商业建议 – 更高的公司收入
      • [employees making decisions] – [better business suggestions] – [more business revenue]
    • 参考论证2：员工参与决策 – 满足顾客需求 – 顾客认可度
      • [involving employees in the decision-making process] – [meeting clients’ demand] – [customers’ recognition]
缺点
  • 论点1：产生上下级矛盾
    • Employees participating in the decision-making process may create conflicts between employees and managers.
    • 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 意见不同和争执 – 老板员工之间的冲突
      • [staff participation] – [disagreement or arguments] – [conflicts among employees and employers]
    • 参考论证2：员工参与决策 – 不同意见 – 管理层和员工之间关系紧张
      • [staff engagement in decision making] – [different opinions] – [tensions between management and employees]
  • 论点2：降低工作效率
    • Employees engaged in the decision-making process will cause a decrease in working efficiency.
    • 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 复杂的协商流程 – 浪费时间
      • [employee involvement in decision making] – [complex negotiation procedures] – [waste of time]
    • 参考论证2：员工参与决策 – 冗长的讨论时间 – 拖慢工作进度
      • [employee engagement in the decision process] – [longer discussion time] – [slower work progress]

  • Paragraph 1 — Introduction

    In today’s rapidly evolving world, many issues continue to attract public attention and debate. One such issue is whether involving employees in the decision-making process of products and services brings more advantages or disadvantages. While different people hold different opinions regarding this matter, my view is that there are both advantages and disadvantages of such a policy. This essay will discuss the key aspects of the topic and explain why this perspective is reasonable through logical arguments and relevant examples.

  • Paragraph 2 — Advantages

    To begin with, one major advantage of involving employees in decision-making is that it can result in high employee loyalty. This means that employees making decisions can lead to more engagement in work, which further results in a sense of belonging. For instance, involving employees in the decision-making process can bring about their motivation and passion, ultimately leading to higher employment loyalty, which clearly illustrates the positive impact. Therefore, this argument strongly supports the idea that employee participation enhances workplace commitment.

  • Paragraph 3 — Disadvantages

    However, there are also notable disadvantages. One significant concern is that employees participating in the decision-making process may create conflicts between employees and managers. This is because staff participation can result in disagreement or arguments, which leads to conflicts among employees and employers. For example, staff engagement in decision making can contribute to different opinions, bringing about tensions between management and employees demonstrates the negative impact. Despite these drawbacks, a balanced understanding of both sides remains essential for employee involvement in decision-making.

  • Paragraph 4 — Conclusion

    In conclusion, employee participation in organizational decision-making is a complex issue with both benefits and drawbacks. After examining the main arguments and supporting evidence, it is clear that while it increases employee loyalty, it may also create workplace conflicts. Ultimately, adopting a thoughtful and balanced understanding of this topic will help individuals and society make better decisions in the future. +1

书面考试是否有效评估学习成果 #101005 极高频 极限预测

The formal written examination can be a valid method to assess students’ learning. To what extent do you agree or disagree? Use your own experience. 正式的笔试是评估学生学习成绩的有效方法。你在多大程度上同意或不同意? 运用你自己的经验。

观点1：是有效方法

Personally speaking, I firmly believe that a formal written examination can be a valid method to assess students’ learning.

  • 论点1：准确评估知识掌握程度
    • A formal written examination can lead to a clear measurement of knowledge mastery.
    • 参考论证1：笔试 – 数字化成绩 – 学习结果准确评估
      • [formal written examination] – [quantified scores] – [accurate assessment of learning outcomes]
    • 参考论证2：笔试 – 数字化考试结果 – 学业表现准确评估
      • [written examinations] – [numerical test outcomes] – [precise evaluation of academic performance]
  • 论点2：客观测试学习成果
    • A formal written examination can ensure removal of subjectivity in grading.
    • 参考论证1：笔试 – 考试形式统一 – 无主观偏差
      • [formal written examination] – [identical test formats] – [bias-free evaluation]
    • 参考论证2：笔试 – 考试流程统一客观学术评价
      • [written examinations] – [standardized procedures] – [objective academic evaluation]
  • 论点3：全面测试学习成果
    • Formal written examination can result in comprehensive understanding of students’ learning outcomes.
    • 参考论证1：笔试 – 精细的题目设计 – 全面检测学生知识漏洞
      • [formal written examination] – [careful exam item design] – [identification of learning deficiencies]
    • 参考论证2：笔试 – 有代表性的题目选取 – 检测学生重点知识点掌握情况
      • [written examination] – [representative question selection] – [assessment of core concept mastery]
观点2：不是有效方法

Personally speaking, I firmly believe that a formal written examination is not a valid method to assess students’ learning.

  • 论点1：无法检验实践能力
    • Formal written examination can have inability to assess practical ability development.
    • 参考论证1：笔试 – 小组项目不足 – 难易检测团队合作力
      • [formal written examination] – [few group projects] – [failure to evaluate teamwork skills]
    • 参考论证2：笔试 – 注重理论 – 无法判断实践能力
      • [written examinations] – [focus on theory] – [limited measurement of problem-solving ability]
  • 论点2：成绩失真
    • Formal written examination can bring about distorted results under pressure.
    • 参考论证1：笔试 – 备考焦虑 – 无法反映实际水平
      • [formal written examination] – [exam anxiety] – [failure to reflect true mastery]
    • 参考论证2：笔试 – 考试时间紧张 – 匆忙作答/未完成作答
      • [written examinations] – [strict exam time limits] – [rushed responses / incomplete answers]
  • 论点3：不计过程（只重结果）
    • Formal written examination can lead to neglect of the learning process evaluation.
    • 参考论证1：笔试 – 应试记忆 – 无法审核是否知识内化
      • [formal written examination] – [test-oriented memorization] – [lack of assessing conceptual understanding]
    • 参考论证2：笔试 – 死记硬背 – 无法检验知识理解
      • [written examination] – [exam cramming] – [insufficient assessment of knowledge comprehension]

  • Paragraph 1 — Introduction

    In today’s rapidly evolving world, many issues continue to attract public attention and debate. One such issue is whether a formal written examination can be a valid method to assess students’ learning. While different people hold different opinions regarding this matter, my view is that a formal written examination can indeed be a valid method to assess students’ learning. This essay will discuss the key aspects of the topic and explain why this perspective is reasonable through logical arguments and relevant examples.

  • Paragraph 2 — First Main Argument

    To begin with, one major aspect of formal written examinations is that they can lead to a clear measurement of knowledge mastery. This means that formal written examination can lead to quantified scores, which further results in accurate assessment of learning outcomes. For instance, written examinations can bring about numerical test outcomes, ultimately leading to precise evaluation of academic performance, which clearly illustrates the importance of this factor. Therefore, this argument strongly supports the idea that written exams accurately evaluate knowledge mastery.

  • Paragraph 3 — Second Main Argument

    Secondly, another significant consideration is that a formal written examination can ensure removal of subjectivity in grading. This is because formal written examination can result in identical test formats, which leads to bias-free evaluation. For example, written examinations can contribute to standardized procedures, bringing about objective academic evaluation demonstrates how this aspect affects the issue. Even though some may argue that formal written examinations have an inability to assess practical ability development, the overall evidence suggests that written exams remain a valid and reliable assessment method.

  • Paragraph 4 — Conclusion

    In conclusion, the role of formal written examinations in assessing student learning is a complex issue with multiple perspectives. After examining the main arguments and supporting evidence, it is clear that written exams are effective in accurately measuring knowledge mastery and ensuring objective evaluation. Ultimately, adopting a thoughtful and balanced understanding of this topic will help individuals and society make better decisions in the future. +1
