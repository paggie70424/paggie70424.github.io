### 法律改变人的行为 #101003 极高频 极限预测

::: tabs

@tab Question

Some people think human behaviors can be changed by laws, while others think laws have little effect. What is your opinion.
一些人认为法律可以限制人的行为，另一些人认为法律没有什么作用。你的意见是什么?

::: details 观点1：可以限制
Personally speaking, human behaviors can be changed by laws.
* 论点1：防止犯罪
  - Laws can prevent people from taking part in crime.
  * 参考论证1：法律 – 惩罚 – 避免犯罪
    - [legislations] – [punishments and penalties] – [reduced criminal behavior]
  * 参考论证2：法律 – 定义犯罪行为 – 避免犯法
    - [rules and regulations] – [definition of criminal behaviors] – [the avoidance of crime]
* 论点2：正确价值观
  - Laws can help shape citizens’ sense of morality and influence their behavior.
  * 参考论证1：法律 – 定义道德标准 – 道德行为
    - [laws and legislations] – [moral standards] – [ethical conduct]
  * 参考论证2：法律 – 清晰的行为准则 – 遵守法律的行为
    - [rules and regulations] – [clear behavioral boundaries] – [lawful compliance]
* 论点3：承担社会责任
  - Laws can guide people to assume their social responsibilities.
  * 参考论证1：法律 – 建立社会责任感 – 对社会负责的行为
    - [laws and legislations] – [a sense of public duty] – [responsible conduct]
  * 参考论证2：法律 – 法律义务 – 实现法律责任
    - [rules and regulations] – [legal obligations] – [the fulfilment of civic duties]

:::

::: details 观点2：不能限制
Personally speaking, laws have little effect on human behaviors.
* 论点1：法律约束无法限制内在动机
  - Legal control have limited capacity to regulate people’s internal motivations and behaviors.
  * 参考论证1：法律 – 建立社会责任感 – 对社会负责的行为
    - [laws and legislation] – [external constraints] – [limited influence on personal choices]
  * 参考论证2：法律 – 法律义务 – 实现法律责任
    - [rules and regulation] – [limited fear of punishiment] – [temporary compliance]
* 论点2：法律缺乏执行力
  - Law’s limited enforcement reduces the ability to regulate behavior.
  - 参考论证1：法律 – 执行力有限 – 震慑作用低
    - [laws and legislation] – [enforcement gaps] – [low deterrent effect]
  - 参考论证2：法律 – 监管能力不足 – 规避合法行为
    - [rules and regulation] – [weak supervision] – [rule avoidance]
- 论点3：法律不能预防犯罪行为
  - Laws are unable to prevent legal violation.
  - 参考论证1：法律 – 以惩罚为主的机制 – 预防作用有限
    - [laws and legislation] – [punishment-based measures] – [weak preventive effect]
  - 参考论证2：法律 – 事后型执行方式 – 行为预防能力不足
    - [rules and regulation] – [reactive enforcement] – [limited behavioral prevention]

:::

@tab Answer

* Paragraph 1 — Introduction

  In today’s rapidly evolving world, many issues continue to attract public attention and debate. One such issue is <span style="color:orange">whether human behaviors can be changed by laws, or if laws have little effect</span>. While different people hold different opinions regarding this matter, my view is that <span style="color:orange">human behaviors can indeed be changed by laws</span>. This essay will discuss the key aspects of the topic and explain why this perspective is reasonable through logical arguments and relevant examples.

* Paragraph 2 — First Main Argument

  To begin with, one major aspect of <span style="color:orange">legal influence</span> is <span style="color:orange">its ability to prevent people from taking part in crime</span>. This means that <span style="color:orange">through legislations, punishments, and penalties, the legal system can lead to reduced criminal behavior</span>. For instance, <span style="color:orange">rules and regulations that provide a clear definition of criminal behaviors help citizens ensure the avoidance of crime</span>, which clearly illustrates the importance of this factor. Therefore, this argument strongly supports the idea that <span style="color:orange">laws serve as an effective deterrent</span>.

* Paragraph 3 — Second Main Argument

  Secondly, another significant consideration is <span style="color:orange">that laws can help shape citizens' sense of morality and influence their behavior</span>. This is because <span style="color:orange">laws and legislations define moral standards which lead to ethical conduct</span>. For example, <span style="color:orange">rules and regulations establishing clear behavioral boundaries result in lawful compliance</span> demonstrates how this aspect affects the issue. Even though some may argue that <span style="color:orange">legal controls have limited capacity to regulate internal motivations</span>, the overall evidence suggests that laws guide people to assume <span style="color:orange">their social responsibilities</span>.

* Paragraph 4 — Conclusion

  In conclusion, <span style="color:orange">the impact of laws on human behavior</span> is a complex issue with multiple perspectives. After examining the main arguments and supporting evidence, it is clear that <span style="color:orange">laws are effective in preventing crime and shaping moral standards</span>. Ultimately, adopting a thoughtful and balanced understanding of this topic will help individuals and society make better decisions in the future.

  - Involving employees in the decision-making process of products and services will lead to better business performance.
  * 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 更好的商业建议 – 更高的公司收入
    - [employees making decisions] – [better business suggestions] – [more business revenue]
  * 参考论证2：员工参与决策 – 满足顾客需求 – 顾客认可度
    - [involving employees in the decision-making process] – [meeting clients' demand] – [customers' recognition]

:::

::: details 缺点

  * 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 意见不同和争执 – 老板员工之间的冲突

    - [staff engagement in decision making] – [different opinions] – [tensions between management and employees]
* 论点2：降低工作效率
  - Employees engaged in the decision-making process will cause a decrease in working efficiency.
  * 参考论证1：员工参与决策 – 复杂的协商流程 – 浪费时间
    - [employee involvement in decision making] – [complex negotiation procedures] – [waste of time]
  * 参考论证2：员工参与决策 – 冗长的讨论时间 – 拖慢工作进度
    - [employee engagement in the decision process] – [longer discussion time] – [slower work progress]

  - A formal written examination can lead to a clear measurement of knowledge mastery.

  * 参考论证1：笔试 – 考试形式统一 – 无主观偏差
    - [formal written examination] – [identical test formats] – [bias-free evaluation]

    - [formal written examination] – [careful exam item design] – [identification of learning deficiencies]
  * 参考论证2：笔试 – 有代表性的题目选取 – 检测学生重点知识点掌握情况
    - [written examination] – [representative question selection] – [assessment of core concept mastery]

:::

  - Formal written examination can have inability to assess practical ability development.
  * 参考论证1：笔试 – 小组项目不足 – 难易检测团队合作力
    - [formal written examination] – [few group projects] – [failure to evaluate teamwork skills]
  * 参考论证2：笔试 – 注重理论 – 无法判断实践能力
    - [written examinations] – [focus on theory] – [limited measurement of problem-solving ability]
* 论点2：成绩失真
  - Formal written examination can bring about distorted results under pressure.
  * 参考论证1：笔试 – 备考焦虑 – 无法反映实际水平
    - [formal written examination] – [exam anxiety] – [failure to reflect true mastery]
  * 参考论证2：笔试 – 考试时间紧张 – 匆忙作答/未完成作答
    - [written examinations] – [strict exam time limits] – [rushed responses / incomplete answers]
* 论点3：不计过程（只重结果）
  - Formal written examination can lead to neglect of the learning process evaluation.
  * 参考论证1：笔试 – 应试记忆 – 无法审核是否知识内化
    - [formal written examination] – [test-oriented memorization] – [lack of assessing conceptual understanding]
  * 参考论证2：笔试 – 死记硬背 – 无法检验知识理解
    - [written examination] – [exam cramming] – [insufficient assessment of knowledge comprehension]

:::
