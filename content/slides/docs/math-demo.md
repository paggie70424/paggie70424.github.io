## 块级公式

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

---

## 微积分

$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}
$$

---

## 矩阵

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
ax + by \\
cx + dy
\end{pmatrix}
$$
